Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Harbour Tech Solutions, Mumbai, India

Summary: We collect only what we need, we never sell your data, and you can request deletion at any time by contacting us at support@harbourtech.com.

1. Who We Are

Harbour Tech Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") is a technology consulting company based in Mumbai, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact form: Your name, email address, phone number, and message when you submit an inquiry.
  • Account registration: Your full name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited and time spent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Manage your account and authenticate your identity
  • Send you updates about your projects or our services (only if you've opted in)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage

Currently, account data (name, email, password) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any external server at this stage. As we expand our services, we will update this policy to reflect any changes to our data storage practices.

Contact form submissions are currently handled client-side. Once our backend is integrated, submissions will be securely stored on our servers located in India.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or a court order
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Harbour Tech Solutions or our users
  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating our website, under strict confidentiality agreements

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal browser storage (localStorage) for theme preferences and session management. We do not currently use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@harbourtech.com.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
