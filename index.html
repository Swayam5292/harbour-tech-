FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

How long does a typical project take? ▼
Timelines vary by complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks. Full-stack applications typically take 6-12 weeks. Enterprise-grade platforms with AI integrations can take 3-5 months. We always provide a detailed timeline in our project proposal before you commit.
Do you offer support after the project is delivered? ▼
Yes. All plans include a post-launch support window — 1 week for Starter, 30 days for Growth. Enterprise clients get 24/7 priority support with a dedicated SLA. We also offer ongoing maintenance retainers for long-term partnerships.
Can I see the project progress during development? ▼
Absolutely. We work in 2-week Agile sprints and schedule a demo call at the end of each sprint. You'll have access to a staging environment throughout the build so you can review progress in real-time, not just at the end.
What technologies do you specialize in? ▼
Our team is proficient in React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, GCP, and Azure. For AI/ML, we work with TensorFlow, PyTorch, and OpenAI APIs. We choose the best stack for your specific requirements.
Is the Starter plan suitable for a startup MVP? ▼
Yes — the Starter plan is specifically designed for MVPs and validation-stage products. It covers up to 5 pages/screens, responsive design, basic SEO, and is delivered in 2 weeks. It's the fastest way to get a professional product in front of your users and investors.
Do you sign NDAs and IP ownership agreements? ▼
Yes, always. We sign a mutual NDA before any discovery call. Upon project completion and final payment, all intellectual property is fully transferred to you. We retain no rights to your codebase, data, or business logic.
